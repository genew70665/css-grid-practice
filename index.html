Welcome to Our Gallery

Hi! I'm Faye. I have lived in the Lake Charles (Calcaalsie) area for 50 yesrs. Married for 43 years. I have three sons, and four grand chilren. Stained glass is more than a hobby for me. I love the satisfaction of creating beautiful pieces that I know that will bring many years of enjoyment.

What movtivated you to started making the stained glass?


We were building a new house, and I wanted to buy a glass panel for the house. The panel costs over $3000. I thought I can make my own much cheaper. I started looking for a class to learn


Well that answer leads right into our next question.

How did you learn to make stained glass art


I found a class at McNeese Univeristy. The class was taught by Frank Thompson. Franks Thompson built houses, and he would give one of his panels with each house. Unfortunately, Frank Thompson passed away earlier this year.
